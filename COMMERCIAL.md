`@effindomv2/fui-rs` is licensed under AGPL-3.0-only for open-source use. A
separate commercial license is available for proprietary products that cannot
meet the AGPL's source-sharing requirements.

## Who needs a commercial license?

You need a commercial license if all of the following are true:

- You are building a product distributed or provided to customers, including
  SaaS, desktop applications, kiosks, embedded UIs, or licensed internal tools.
- You do not want to release your product under AGPL-3.0-only.
- Your use is not otherwise permitted by the AGPL.

Open-source projects that comply with AGPL-3.0-only can use FUI-RS without a
commercial license.

## What the commercial license covers

- Use of `@effindomv2/fui-rs` in proprietary, closed-source products.
- No requirement to publish the proprietary product's source code under AGPL.
- Project-scoped pricing rather than a one-size-fits-all rate.

The EffinDom runtime is a separately published package with its own license;
this FUI-RS commercial license does not redefine the runtime's terms.
